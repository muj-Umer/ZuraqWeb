The Pattern Story

Two Traditions,
One Language

The geometric DNA of this hoodie speaks a shared visual language — traced from the blue-and-white tilework of Lisbon to the mosaic splendor of the Middle East.

Portuguese Azulejo

Born in 15th-century Lisbon, azulejo tilework transforms walls into infinite geometric gardens. These patterns carry centuries of Moorish mathematical influence.

Middle Eastern Geometry

Islamic geometric art transforms the infinite into the tangible — complex star patterns and interlocking tessellations that reveal new depth the longer you look.

The Zuraq Fusion

Six months of research distilled into tonal embroidery that blends both visual languages — honoring both origins while belonging to the present.
